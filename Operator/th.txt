Three type of operator 
1. unary operator
    - it will required one operand
    ex : a++ ,++a 
2. binary operator
    - it will required two operand
3. ternary operator
    - it will required three operand 

binary operator :

4. logical operator  : && , || , !
5. bitwise operator : & , | , ^ , << , >>
